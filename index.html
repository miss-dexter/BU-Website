Who are we?

BU is an abbreviation for Be You. BU is an app designed for people who identify as LGBTQ+. The idea is to reach out to people that are facing mental health issues by providing them with an outlet and a platform that helps curb their mental health issues.

Our services

Counselling services

One of the key features of BU is its counselling feature, which provides a safe and confidential space for individuals who identify as LGBTQ+ to connect with professional counsellors. The counselling feature offers personalized support and guidance for a range of mental health concerns, including anxiety, depression, relationship issues, and more. Through the app, users can schedule appointments with licensed therapists and connect with them via live consultation or chat. The counselling sessions are tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual, taking into consideration their sexual orientation and gender identity. BU's counselling feature is also equipped with tools to help users track their progress and set goals to achieve positive mental health outcomes. Users can track their mood, access resources and self-help tips to manage their mental health.
